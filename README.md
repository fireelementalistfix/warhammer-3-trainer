## 🤖 Features: What This Thing Actually Does *In-Game*

Ready to bend the Winds of Magic to your will? Here are the key modules of the trainer. Every toggle is designed to be **hotkey-activated** or toggled via a clean on-screen overlay.

- **Gold Hub – Press (F1)**  
  Toggles **Infinite Treasury**. Every time your faction's gold dips below 5,000, it re-ups to exactly 10,000. Perfect for "oops I recruited four stacks of Chosen" moments.

- **March Forth – Press (F2)**      
  Toggles **Unlimited Movement Range** for every army you control. Run from Naggaroth to the Southlands in two turns. Your supply lines might weep, but your Tomb Kings will appreciate the road trip.

- **Colder and Wiser – Press (F3)**   
  Toggles **God Mode for Units**. Selected units in any of your armies (heroes or lords included) will regenerate health rapidly. Great for seeing Ungrim Ironfist tank four Soul Grinders for lulz.

- **Anno Domini Builder – Press (F4)**  
  Toggles **Instant Construction**. Any building you queue now completes in zero turns. Myrmidia's Cathedral completed instantly? Yes.

- **Cooldown Cascade – Press (F5)**   
  Toggles **Zero Ability Cooldowns** for all lords & heroes in the selected army. Enjoy chain-snaring Skarbrand’s "Unfettered Fury" or chain-casting with Teclis without waiting.

- **Diplomacy Booster (Press F6)**   
  Toggles a +40 **global relation modifier** with every other faction. Also your "confederation attraction" has a higher roll chance. Playing a Dawi? Unite the Karaz Ankor with fewer Runes.

- **Population Bomb (F7)** – *For Skaven & Cultist factions*  
  Adds +75 to your **Under-Empire/Chaos Cult resource (Follower/Scheming Points) per turn**. Skrolk will be thrilled.

- **Toggle UI Hotkey** – For screenshots! F8 toggles the Trainer's overlay ON or OFF.

All features are **safe** for single-player campaigns. Nothing is injected game-side until you press the specific hotkey. And you can open and close the Trainer mid-campaign as many times as you want — no save corruption unless you rely on zero-turn to beat survival battles.
